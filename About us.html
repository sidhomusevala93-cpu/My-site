Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver **royalty-free creativity to all**. We are dedicated to providing a boundless, high-quality, and completely open visual library, ensuring that artistic expression is accessible and effortless for everyone.

Our Vision

To become the world's leading creative resource for free stock media. We envision a future where every creator, regardless of skill or budget, has access to the tools they need to bring their ideas to life without compromise.

Our Story

2018: The Spark

Ahmad Raza conceives the idea for a free, high-quality stock photo platform after struggling to find reliable assets for his projects.

2019: PIXORA Launch

The first version of PIXORA goes live with an initial library of 100,000 images, focusing on user-friendly design and simple licensing.

2021: Expanding the Library

We hit the 1 million image milestone, thanks to our growing community of photographers and our dedicated curation team.

2023: Trusted by Creators

PIXORA is now used by over 200,000 creators worldwide, solidifying our place as a go-to resource for royalty-free content.

What is PIXORA?

PIXORA is a creative hub for **royalty-free images, videos, vectors, and more**. We provide a vast library of high-quality assets contributed by a passionate global community. Unlike traditional stock photo sites, our platform is built on the principle of accessibility and freedom.

Our mission is to empower creators of all kinds—from graphic designers and marketers to students and developers. With a user-friendly interface and a commitment to quality, we ensure you can find the perfect visual to tell your story, completely free of charge.
