Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PIXORA, from image uploads to licensing and more.

How do I upload images to my gallery?

To upload images, you must have an active PIXORA account. Navigate to the "Upload" section in your dashboard, select the images from your device, and follow the on-screen prompts to add titles, tags, and descriptions.

What file types are supported for upload?

We support a wide range of image formats, including **JPG, PNG, GIF, SVG, and WEBP**. We also accept **PSD and AI** files for our premium users to allow for more flexible design options.

Can I download images in different resolutions?

Yes, all users can download images. Depending on the license and the original image quality, you can choose from various resolutions and sizes to fit your needs, from web-optimized to high-resolution print-ready files.

How can I find a specific image?

Our powerful search functionality allows you to find images using keywords, categories, and tags. You can also filter results by color, orientation (horizontal/vertical), and image type.

What is a royalty-free license?

A royalty-free license means you can use an image multiple times for a single, one-time payment. You don't have to pay additional fees for each use. Our licenses are simple and cover most uses, but always check the specific license terms for each image.

What are the different types of licenses available?

We offer two primary licenses: **Standard** and **Extended**. The Standard license is suitable for most digital and non-commercial projects, while the Extended license covers commercial use, merchandise, and larger print runs.

How do I share images with others?

You can share images directly from our platform using the share button on each image's page. We support sharing to popular social media platforms and provide a direct link for easy embedding.

Can I create my own albums or collections?

Yes! With your PIXORA account, you can create unlimited albums to organize your favorite images. Simply click the "Add to Album" button on any image page and select an existing album or create a new one.

What should I do if I find inappropriate content?

We take content moderation very seriously. If you encounter any content that violates our terms of service, please use the **"Report Image"** button on the image page. Our moderation team will review the report and take appropriate action.

Can I use the images for my business or commercial projects?

Yes, many of our images are available for commercial use. Look for images with a **Commercial License** badge. For more details on what's covered, please refer to our full licensing terms.

How do the social features like likes and comments work?

You can "like" an image to show your appreciation and add it to your favorites. Comments allow you to engage with the artist and other users, sharing feedback or asking questions about the work. Be sure to follow our community guidelines for respectful interactions.

Is the website mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! PIXORA is designed to be fully responsive. You can browse, upload, and manage your images on any device, from a desktop computer to a smartphone or tablet.

Are there limits on how many images I can upload or download?

Our free accounts have a monthly limit on uploads and high-resolution downloads. Premium and Pro subscriptions offer unlimited uploads and downloads, along with additional features. You can find more details on our pricing page.

How do I upgrade or manage my subscription?

You can manage your subscription directly from your user dashboard. Simply log in, navigate to the "Billing" or "Subscription" section, and follow the steps to upgrade or update your plan.

Do you automatically optimize images after I upload them?

Yes, we automatically generate optimized versions of your images for fast loading on the web while keeping the original file for high-resolution downloads. This ensures a great viewing experience for all users.

What's the best resolution for uploading images?

We recommend uploading images that are at least **1920px** on the longest side to ensure they look sharp and can be used for various purposes. Higher resolutions are always better for print-ready downloads.

Can I change the website's theme?

Yes! We offer a **dark mode** option to make browsing easier on your eyes. Just click the moon icon in the header to switch between light and dark themes.

How can I contact customer support?

If you can't find the answer to your question here, you can reach our support team through the **Contact Us** page. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours.

What are your support hours?

Our customer support team is available from Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (EST). We are closed on weekends and public holidays.

Is it free to create an account?

Yes, it's completely free to create an account and start browsing and downloading images with a standard license. Paid plans are available for more advanced features and commercial use.

How is content moderated on the platform?

All uploaded images undergo a manual and automated review process to ensure they comply with our community guidelines and are free from copyright infringement and inappropriate content.

Can I sell my own photos on PIXORA?

We are a royalty-free image gallery, not a marketplace. Our platform is for sharing and using images freely, but we do not currently have a feature for users to sell their own photos directly.

What is an image gallery website?

An image gallery website is a platform where users can browse, upload, and download a curated collection of photos and digital artwork. These sites often provide tools for searching, organizing, and sharing content.

How do I delete my PIXORA account?

You can delete your account by navigating to the "Account Settings" section in your dashboard and following the "Delete Account" instructions. Be aware that this action is permanent and will remove all your uploaded images and data.
