Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 16, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PIXORA, an image gallery and sharing platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our website and services. By using PIXORA, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect both **personal** and **non-personal** data to provide and improve our services.

  • **Personal Data**: This includes information you voluntarily provide when you create an account, such as your name, email address, and profile picture. We also collect data related to your interactions, such as comments and likes.
  • **Image Content**: When you upload images, we collect the image files and any associated metadata (EXIF data, titles, tags, descriptions) you provide.
  • **Non-Personal Data**: We automatically collect information about your device and how you interact with our site. This includes your IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, and search queries. This data helps us analyze user behavior and optimize our services.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to operate, maintain, and enhance the PIXORA platform. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • **Account Management**: Create and manage your user account, allowing you to log in and access your personal gallery.
  • **Service Provision**: Process image uploads, downloads, and the creation of albums and collections.
  • **Search and Discovery**: Use image metadata and your search history to improve the relevance of search results and recommend content.
  • **Communication**: Send you service announcements, updates, and marketing communications (with your consent).
  • **Security**: Monitor for fraudulent activity, unauthorized use, and enforce our Terms of Service.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience on our site. **Cookies** are small data files stored on your device that allow us to remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and analyze site traffic. We use:

  • **Essential Cookies**: Necessary for the website to function properly.
  • **Analytics Cookies**: Help us understand how users interact with our site, enabling us to improve performance and user experience.
  • **Advertising Cookies**: Used by our advertising partners to show you relevant ads based on your interests.

5. Image Content Policies and Public Visibility

When you upload an image, it becomes part of the PIXORA gallery. Unless you specifically set an image to private, all uploaded images and their associated metadata (title, tags, description) are **publicly visible** to all users and visitors. We reserve the right to remove any content that violates our community guidelines or intellectual property policies. You are responsible for ensuring that you have the right to upload any image content you provide to PIXORA.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share information with:

  • **Third-Party Service Providers**: We use trusted third parties to perform functions on our behalf, such as web hosting, payment processing, and analytics (e.g., Google Analytics). These providers have access to your data only as necessary to perform their services.
  • **Advertising Partners**: We may share non-personal data with advertising networks to display targeted advertisements on our site.
  • **Social Media Platforms**: If you use social media features on our site, your data may be shared with those platforms according to their privacy policies.

7. Legal Disclosure and Law Enforcement

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to:

  • Comply with a legal obligation or court order.
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of PIXORA, our users, or the public.
  • Investigate potential violations of our Terms of Service.

8. Security of Your Data

The security of your data is paramount. We use a variety of security measures, including encryption and access controls, to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal data:

  • **Access and Update**: You can access and update your account information directly from your user profile settings.
  • **Deletion**: You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data. Please note that some data may be retained for legal or operational purposes.
  • **Data Portability**: You can request a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

11. International Users and Data Transfer

PIXORA is operated and managed in the United States. If you are a user from outside the U.S., your information will be processed and stored in the U.S., where data protection laws may differ from those in your country. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your data to the U.S.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date." We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.

13. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • **Email**: privacy@pixora.com
  • **Mailing Address**: PIXORA Privacy Team, 123 Gallery Lane, Creativesville, CA 90210
